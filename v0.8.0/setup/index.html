Hardware setupÂ¶

These pages are for Loihi system administrators or other individuals who are setting up a Loihi system for the first time.

While other parts of Nengo Loihi documentation are written with enough ambiguity that they should work on a variety of different setups, this page is specific to certain hardware designs and has complete explicit steps. As such, these instructions are not likely to work with other designs, and as these designs and other infrastructure change, these instructions may no longer work. If you run into problems, please contact the hardware distributor (usually Intel).
